ABOUT

Kyle Richer is an illustrator, graphic designer, and photographer from Rhode Island. He is currently based out of South Coast Massachusetts while studying both illustration and graphic design at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. Using illustration, graphic design, and photography, Kyle strives to combine the three to not only convey important information, but to also explore narratives of the mundane that are often overlooked. For commissions, inquiries, and any other questions contact me at kricher97@gmail.com
